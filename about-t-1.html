Introduction

The VOLFERDA Industry Limited is belong to Guangdong Zhongke Suda Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd in Foshan, China, which is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the research, development, production and sales of capsule house and tiny house.


Volferda is committed to providing sleek, stylish, and smart micro-hotel housing for outdoor travel and travelers to getaway from urban areas. Volferda develops an innovative, high-design, self-contained cabin that uses prefab structural components to reduce disturbance to the site and minimize environmental impact while outfitted with a curated collection of technology and comfort. Offering unique opportunities for guests to commune with nature and wake up to breathtaking views.



Over the past few years, Volferda has focused on the international market and is currently exporting to dozens of countries such as the United States, Canada, Australia, Bulgaria, Russia, Indonesia, etc. We also have local sales representatives and showrooms in the United States, Australia, Russia, Bulgaria, Greece, Saudi Arabia and other countries.


With years of technical expertise and industry experience, we specialize in providing high-quality, environmentally friendly and sustainable mobile home solutions for cultural tourism resorts, boutique residences, public infrastructure and commercial projects.

We pride ourselves not only on being a leader in the industry, but also on inspiring more ambitious teams to join the field and work together to drive the development and evolution of mobile homes. In the future, Volferda will continue to explore innovation and bring more amazing mobile home designs to the table.


Factory

Volferda factory is located in Foshan, Guangdong, China. With a 30,000 square meter factory and 200 workers, the company employs an ERP and MES production management execution system, which allows it to produce up to 100 houses per month while reducing lead times to 20-25 days. 


Volferda offers a full suite of design and development solutions for large-scale projects and is availablein both complete and disassembled shipments, with disassembled shipments that will reduce the cost of shipping for high volume purchases.
