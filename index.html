ABOUT VOLFERDA

Volferda is dedicated to offering a sophisticated and smart mobile hotel solution

for hotel, resort, and airbnb. 

Volferda develops an innovative, high-design, self-contained cabin that uses prefab

 impact. Volferda houses provide travelers with the appeal of "living in the scenery.

How to Buy

Constuction

Contact us by any convenient method and get full information about the project. You can ask our company specialists any questions, find out about the cost, options, shipping and installation or anything.


Contract and Payment
After choosing the house model, we will sign a contract. The contract will include purchase conditions, optional products, water and electricity standards, payment terms and delivery date. Before starting production, you need to pay 50% of the total cost as a deposit.

Production
We manufacture houses in stages: frame, insulation, utilities and finishing. Depending on the complexity of the project, the production process takes 30-50 days. Each client can visit the production process of the house and see that we strictly adhere to standards and ensure the quality of our work.
Foundation Preparation

While your house is being manufactured, you need to prepare the foundation for it at the same time. We will provide you with detailed instructions and the necessary drawings. At the same time, plan water, electricity and other systems in advance.


Delivery and Installation
The house can be delivery in the whole house or parts. You can arrange for a freight forwarder to deliver it. If deliveryed in the whole house, the installation is very simple and can be done in a few hours. If deliveryed in house parts, you can assemble it with detailed drawings and video instructions or use our partners to assemble it.
